About Arthur Morgan

Arthur Morgan is the main protagonist of Red Dead Redemption 2, a senior member of the Van der Linde gang, and a trusted right-hand man to Dutch. Born in 1863, Arthur was raised into the gang life from a young age and became one of the most capable and feared outlaws in the West.

Personality

Arthur is often portrayed as a conflicted man—tough and ruthless as an outlaw, yet compassionate and loyal to those he cares about. His moral choices throughout the story shape how he is remembered: either as a redeemed man seeking peace, or as a ruthless outlaw to the very end.

Legacy

Arthur Morgan’s journey is considered one of the greatest character arcs in video game history. His choices influence John Marston’s future and set the stage for the events of the original Red Dead Redemption. Fans admire Arthur for his depth, humanity, and unforgettable story.
